How to reserve and loan a book

  1. Browse books and make a selection(s).
  2. Add the book(s) to your cart.
  3. Do you have an account with us?
    • If you have an account with us, please log in to your account.
    • If you do not have an account with yet, please sign up for a free account and get your library card today!
      • Once you've created an account, please log in to your account.
  4. Once logged in to your account, you can reserve the book(s). Click the reserve button.
  5. Once you successfully reserved the book(s), you will receive an email confirmation for your reservation.
  6. Your will receive another email with instructions regarding book(s) pickup once ready.
